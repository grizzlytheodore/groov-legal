This Privacy Policy describes how StudioTCHoldings LLC (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Groov mobile application and related services (collectively, the “Service”).

By using Groov, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

Depending on how you use the Service, we may collect:

  • Account information (optional): If the app supports account creation or sign-in, we may collect your email address and basic profile details.
  • Support communications: When you contact us at support@groovvinyl.com, we collect the information you provide (email, message content, attachments).

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Service, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage data: Interactions with the app (e.g., scans, searches, viewed albums, playback actions), crash logs, and basic diagnostic information.
  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, app version, language, and approximate region (not precise GPS location).
  • Log data: IP address (or a truncated/hashed form), timestamps, and technical event logs for security and debugging.

1.3 Images and Scan Content

Groov allows you to capture or upload photos of vinyl covers or labels so the app can identify records and retrieve information. These images may be sent to our backend services and third-party AI services strictly to:

  • Perform image recognition and identify the record or track.
  • Look up metadata (artist, album, track list, etc.) from third-party services.

We do not use these images to identify you personally, and we do not sell or license these images for advertising.

We use the information we collect to:

  • Search for and play music-related videos using YouTube Data API Services.
  • Improve and personalize the user experience.

  • Communicate with you, including responding to support requests.

Groov relies on third-party services to provide core functionality, such as record identification, metadata, and video playback. These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

3.1 YouTube API Services

Groov uses YouTube Data API Services to search for and play music-related videos (for example, full albums or individual tracks). The app may display YouTube video titles, thumbnails, channels, and playback within the app.

By using the Service, you are agreeing to be bound by:

  • YouTube Terms of Service

We do not use YouTube OAuth to access your personal YouTube account, and we do not store your YouTube account credentials, subscriptions, playlists, or viewing history. We only interact with publicly available video data returned by YouTube Data API Services based on search queries generated in the app.

These third-party services are only allowed to use your data as necessary to perform services on our behalf and are obligated to protect it.
