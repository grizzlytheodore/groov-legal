Account & Data Deletion

If you would like to delete your account and associated data from Groov, please email us at:

support@groovvinyl.com

What to include

  • The email address or phone number used to sign up
  • The phrase: “Account Deletion Request”

We will process deletion requests within 7 days.

If you signed in with Apple (“Hide My Email”)

If you used Sign in with Apple and enabled “Hide My Email”, your account may be associated with an Apple relay email address. Relay emails look like:

example@privaterelay.appleid.com

To find your Apple relay email:

  • Open Settings on your iPhone or iPad
  • Tap your name (Apple ID) at the top
  • Tap Sign-In & Security
  • Tap Sign in with Apple
  • Select Groov
  • Your relay email will be shown under Hide My Email

If you do not see Groov listed, you may be signed in with a different Apple ID than the one used to create your account.

What data is deleted

  • User account information
  • Scan history
  • Saved collections and preferences

What data may be retained

We may retain anonymized or aggregated data where legally required.
